Interactive Python Application for Financial Product Tracking at Poncho Capital (WIP)

A User-Friendly, Dynamic Dashboard for Real-Time Investment Analysis and Decision-Making

Introduction

In the fintech industry, it is crucial for organizations to stay updated on the performance of the financial products they offer. Poncho Capital, a fintech company specializing in the purchase and sale of financial products, has recently incorporated a Streamlit application to assist its employees in tracking and making informed decisions about the financial products they provide.

Application Overview

The Streamlit application is designed to monitor various types of financial assets, including Argentinian stocks, CEDEARs (Certificates of Deposit for Argentine Shares), and cryptocurrencies. The application retrieves data from Yahoo Finance and processes it using the yfinance, pandas, plotly, and ta (technical analysis) libraries in Python.

Key Features

  1. 1. Normalized Stock Prices: This feature displays the normalized stock prices of the selected financial products within the specified date range. By normalizing the prices, users can easily compare the performance of different assets, making it simpler to identify trends and potential investment opportunities.
  2. 2. Percentage Change: The application calculates the percentage change of the selected financial products over the specified time frame. This metric helps users quickly evaluate the performance of each asset, making it easier to identify the most promising investment options.
  3. 3. Candlestick Charts with SMA and EMA Indicators: For users interested in technical analysis, the application offers the option to display candlestick charts with Simple Moving Average (SMA) and Exponential Moving Average (EMA) indicators. These charts provide a visual representation of the asset's price movements, allowing users to identify potential entry and exit points

Use Cases

  1. 1. Portfolio Managers: Portfolio managers can use the application to monitor the performance of the assets in their portfolios, track market trends, and make more informed decisions when adjusting their portfolios.
  2. 2. Risk Management Teams: By analyzing the historical price data and technical indicators, risk management teams can identify potential risks and devise strategies to mitigate them.
  3. 3. Investment Analysts: The application can help investment analysts research and compare different financial products to make better recommendations to their clients or internal teams.
  4. 4. Sales and Marketing Teams: By staying updated on the performance of Poncho Capital's financial products, sales and marketing teams can better tailor their strategies and pitches to prospective clients.

Conclusion

Poncho Capital's Streamlit application serves as a powerful tool for monitoring and analyzing the financial products they offer. By providing a user-friendly interface and comprehensive visualizations and metrics, the application empowers Poncho Capital's team members to make well-informed decisions and better serve their clients.

Currently, the application is undergoing enhancements to offer more advanced technical analysis features, as well as a new section dedicated to creating portfolios and calculating the Markowitz Efficient Frontier. This will allow users to apply sophisticated portfolio risk analysis techniques to optimize their investment strategies. Stay tuned for updates on the implementation of these exciting new functionalities in the near future.
